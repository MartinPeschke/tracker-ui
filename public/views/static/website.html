Website Tracking Integration
1

Log into your Convins account or create an account if you do not already have one.

2

Create new account and in platforms select website.

3

Select events you want to track or create custom events.

4

Copy given master script.

5

Paste master script to your website, so that it's loaded with every page. Good place to put it is before body closing tag.

6

Copy first event script and put it on the page associated with your event. I.e. good place for signup event is on the page where users are redirected after they successfully sign up.

8

Repeat step 4 for all choosen events.
